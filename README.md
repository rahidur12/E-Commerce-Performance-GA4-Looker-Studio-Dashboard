## üß† Project Overview

This project focuses on building an **E-Commerce Store Performance Dashboard** using **Looker Studio** to visualize key business metrics, sourced from platforms like Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Ads. The dashboard spans three pages (**Overview**, **Products**, and **Executive KPI**) to provide both strategic and operational depth.

Key areas covered across the pages include:

* **Financial & Marketing KPIs:** Tracking key metrics like **Revenue** ($\$464.85\text{K}$), **Ad Spend** ($\$54.61\text{K}$), **ROAS** ($\$2.29\text{K}$), and **Gross Margin** ($\$410.25\text{K}$).
* **Product Profitability:** Detailed analysis of **Revenue**, **AOV** ($\$639.41$), **Profit Proxy** ($\$410.25\text{K}$), and **Refund Rate** (2.75%).
* **Customer & Conversion:** Insights into **New Customer Rate** (55.4%), **Transactions** (727), and **Conversion Rate by Device Type**.
* **Traffic Optimization:** Breaking down **Revenue and ROAS by Traffic Source** (Paid, Organic, Direct, Referral).
